A little about the developer...


Through the website it is possible to show a little about my hobby, what I love to do and show what I learned during 5 arduous years in the world of athletics. I am a middle distance runner, practicing the sport since 2018, in the 800 and 1500 meter events. I am part of the cultural and sports association of São Judas Tadeu from the Japanese colony, and I am a member of the São Paulo Futebol Club.


I know, just like any other sport, it gets lazy sometimes, and there will always be ups and downs, and running, especially alone, is no different. However, here's a tip, which I saw in the phrase of an Instagram influencer Brendom Bressam, where in all his videos he says “Don't even think, just go”, which helps me to start all my training.


The purpose of the website


I try to show every day my effort and what I absorb from each training session and competition that I practice and participate in, now not just running and practicing the sport but with this website which is much more than just a school project.


I hope you enjoy the training tips and learn how this sport works in the lives of millions of people!
