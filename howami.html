How can I know my performance?

It largely depends on your age, and the training you do every day. It can vary with goals, whether you are elite, whether you run for health, or by age.

If you've already reached the 60 mark, I'm sorry to tell you but times haven't turned out like they did in your youth, but completing the distance, be it 5km or even 15km, congratulations, it's incredible!
